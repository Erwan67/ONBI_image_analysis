Assignments - Complete at least three sections.

1) Fiji Timeseries A

     Examines visualisation of timeseries data in Fiji. Allow around 40 minutes for this.

2) Fiji Timeseries B

     Examines tracking of timeseries data in Fiji. Allow around 40 minutes for this.

3) Object distances for tracking 

     Discuss how a object distance for tracking can be formulated (less that 30 min)

4) MRI Structural Brain Connectivity (Tractography)

    This practical looks at diffusion MRI for tracing structural white-matter pathways in the brain.  Allow around 60 minutes for this. 

5) MRI Functional Brain Connectivity (ICA)

    This practical looks at applying ICA to functional MRI data for estimating functional connectivity.  Allow around 60 minutes for this.
